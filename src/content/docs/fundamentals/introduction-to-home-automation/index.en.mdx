Your home does more than you think. Or at least, it could. This guide is your starting point. No technical background required, no jargon, no assumptions about what you already know.

Over seven chapters, you will learn what home automation actually is, what it can do for you, how to think about it the right way, and how to take your first steps without wasting time or money.

<Callout type="info" title="Who is this guide for?">
  Complete beginners. Even if you have never set up a smart device or are not
  sure what "smart home" really means, this guide is for you.
</Callout>

By the end, you will understand the core ideas, know what to look out for, and have a clear plan for getting started with a setup that respects your privacy and grows with you.
