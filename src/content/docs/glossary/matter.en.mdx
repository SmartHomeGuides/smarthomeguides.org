## What is Matter?

**Matter** (formerly Project CHIP — Connected Home over IP) is an open-source connectivity standard for smart home devices. It aims to ensure interoperability across ecosystems so that a single device can work with Apple HomeKit, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Samsung SmartThings simultaneously.

## Key Features

- **Multi-ecosystem**: One device works with all major platforms
- **Local-first**: Operates over your local network (Wi-Fi and Thread)
- **Secure by design**: Device attestation and encrypted communication
- **IP-based**: Built on standard internet protocols

## Supported Device Types

- Lights and switches
- Smart plugs and outlets
- Door locks
- Thermostats and HVAC
- Window coverings
- Sensors (motion, contact, temperature)
- Media devices (TV, speakers)

## How Matter Works

Matter devices communicate over **Wi-Fi** or **Thread** (a low-power mesh network). A **Matter controller** (your phone, smart speaker, or hub) manages device commissioning and communication.
